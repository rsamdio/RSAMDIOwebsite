About Us

Rotaract South Asia Multi-Districts Information Organization (RSA MDIO)

Rotaract South Asia Multi District Information Organization (RSA MDIO) is a Multi-District Information Organisation (MDIO) serving Rotaract Districts across South Asia.

On 19th July, 2010 PP Karthik Kittu as the Charter President and PP Thansen Pandi as the Charter Secretary laid the foundation of Rotaract South Asia MDIO.

With 8 countries and 46 member districts, RSAMDIO has established itself as the most effective and reliable information body. Bringing together 3900+ Rotaract Clubs, boasting of more than 79,000 reported members, we have always laid the foundation for the districts to have a stage to unite and deliver the most outstanding Multi Districts events in the region.

In South Asia region, we cover 8 countries which comprise 46 Districts including 3,949 active Rotaract Clubs out of which 2,289 are Community Based Clubs & 1,660 are University Based Clubs. As for membership, total members reported in South Asia is 79,165 out of which 38,357 belong to Community Based Clubs & 40,808 belong to University Based Clubs.

Rotaract South Asia MDIO works to strengthen the Rotaract movement in South Asia by providing a platform for Rotaractors to connect and collaborate. It also works closely with Rotary International and other MDIOs to promote Rotary's values and objectives.

Rotaract focuses on the development of young adults as leaders in their communities and workplaces.
